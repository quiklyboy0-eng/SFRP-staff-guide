Staff Standard Operating Procedures

Clear, professional, and consistent rules that keep SFRP running smoothly. Every staff member is expected to know and follow these guidelines.

Zero tolerance for abuse
Always documented
Fair & consistent enforcement

Core Expectations

As a member of SFRP staff, you are a representative of the entire community. These core expectations are non-negotiable and form the foundation of every decision you make.

  • Act professionally at all times
    You represent the server 24/7. Maintain composure, use respectful language, and never let personal emotions affect your duties — even during high-stress situations or player complaints.
  • No bias or favoritism
    Treat every player equally regardless of friendship, in-game status, donations, or history. Decisions must be based solely on facts and rules.
  • Enforce rules consistently
    The same rule violation must result in the same outcome for every player. Inconsistency damages trust in the staff team.
  • All actions must be justified
    Every punishment, warning, or decision must be backed by clear evidence and logged with the exact rule broken and reasoning provided.

Staff Conduct

How you behave while on duty directly affects the player experience and the reputation of the entire staff team.

  • Remain calm and respectful
    De-escalate situations. Never raise your voice or use aggressive language.
  • No arguments with players
    If a player becomes hostile, mute them politely and direct them to a staff ticket or higher-up. Never engage in back-and-forth debates.
  • No trolling or retaliation
    Zero tolerance. Any form of staff trolling, power-tripping, or retaliatory actions will result in immediate removal.
  • Handle all reports objectively
    Base every decision on evidence and server rules — never personal feelings or past interactions.

Communication

  • Use clear and professional language
    Short, polite, and easy to understand — no slang or abbreviations in official interactions.
  • No sarcasm or trolling
    Ever. Players should never feel mocked by staff.
  • Keep staff chats relevant
    Off-topic conversation belongs in #staff-lounge.
  • No leaking internal info
    This includes staff discussions, upcoming updates, or private Discord channels.

Discord Checks

Daily quality checks
Review staff channels and support requests each shift. Report missing or incorrect logs immediately.
Verify roles and permissions
Confirm staff members have the correct tags, access, and channels. Remove improper access without delay.
Monitor public behavior
Staff must be polite and professional in all public Discord areas. Any misconduct is logged and escalated.
Report suspicious DMs
If a staff member receives a questionable direct message, screenshot it and notify senior leadership.

Command Usage

  • Use approved commands only
    Only execute commands listed in staff documentation. Unauthorized command use is prohibited.
  • Confirm command effects
    Before applying punishments, verify syntax and expected result. Avoid mistakes by double-checking.
  • Keep command logs complete
    Record the command issued, target player, reason, and evidence. This is required for audits.
  • No private command testing
    Do not experiment with commands in public channels or on players unless approved by senior staff.
  • No off-duty commands
    Off-duty commands are strictly prohibited and will result in serious disciplinary actions. Off-duty commands (ODC) are restricted to JMGMT+ ranks only.

Uniform Guidelines

  • Wear your staff tag
    Keep the official staff tag visible in-game and on Discord. If the tag is missing, notify leadership immediately.
  • Maintain a professional avatar
    Avoid offensive or inappropriate profile images and names while on duty.
  • Follow role-specific dress codes
    Certain events or enforcement shifts may require a specific appearance. Comply with those instructions.

Search Policy

Searches for player information or logs must be handled carefully and only when authorized.

  • Do not perform searches for personal reasons.
  • Use official search tools and follow written search procedures.
  • Log every search request, including who requested it and why.

Leave of Absence

If you need time away from staff duty, request a Leave of Absence in advance whenever possible.

  • Submit your LOA request with expected dates and reason.
  • Keep communication open during extended leaves.
  • Return only after approval from senio IA+ staff and confirm your status.

Promotions

Promotions are earned through consistent performance, leadership, and dependable execution of staff duties. Below are the specific requirements and limitations for each team.

Team Requirements Max Promotions per Month
Moderator 3 hours of quota required, 6 hours for promotion 5
Administrator 3 trainings and 6 hours for promotion 4
Internal Affairs 4 hours of quota 1 per 2 weeks
Management 3 hours of quota 1 per months
Directive 2 hour required of quota 1 promotion per month

Additional Requirements:

  • Maintain accurate logs, positive communication, and strong teamwork.
  • Mentor newer staff and lead by example.
  • Promotion decisions are based on documented performance and MGMT+ staff review it.
